About

In psychology, or mindfulness, there is a practice of handling lot of thoughts; write it down on a piece of paper and decide a time when to go through the thoughts.

As my note-cube had in mysterious way disappeared, I couldn't apply it in my daily work, and I needed it for work, since a lot of thoughts where popping up at freetime. A normal person would most probably go to a shop and by a new note-cube, but I found out, why not make an app for it instead - more fun :D

Since ideas related to work may be intellectual property, it was utmost important to keep the thoughts safe. I also didn't want to have any online-storage which will cost a small fee, so I ended up to rely on emails

It is intented to use the work account was used to save your thoughts, since then everything is kept in the organization. To use your work-account together with Think-Later, your organization needs to approve it. Therefore, Think-Later will be released as open source project, to simplify that process.

You may add more addresses as possible receivers. This is a way to send ideas to your work-account without the organization doesn't need to approve Think-Later app. If you organization don't let you use it, don't do it and follow their guidelines.

Enjoy the app!
